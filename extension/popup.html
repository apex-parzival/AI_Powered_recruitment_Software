Google Meet overlay
🟢 Panel auto-injects on meet.google.com
Open a Google Meet → the glass panel appears top-right.
  • 1 Start your interview at meet.google.com/new
  • 2 Copy the Meet link → paste in TalentAI to share with candidate
  • 3 Enter your Session ID in the panel's input field
  • 4 Select audio mode: 🎤 Mic+AEC (speakers) or 🖥 System (earphones)
  • 5 Click Start STT — transcript + AI suggestions update live
Make sure TalentAI backend is running on port 8000
